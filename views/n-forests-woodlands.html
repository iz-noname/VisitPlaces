FORESTS & WOODLANDS

Where the adventure begins

Reconciled with nature

Woods and forests are an unexpected treasure, from strolls among ancient trees to a wander through the bluebells. They're also great places for dog walks or cycling that provide family fun and world-class thrills. Here's some of our favourite playgrounds and woodland experiences.

Forests are very diverse, ranging from commercial plantations to native woodlands, to trees and woods in and around our towns and cities. The range of benefits from the forest cover is also diverse, extending beyond basic timber production to encompass employment, bio-diversity, wildlife conservation, environmental protection, rural development, carbon sequestration, amenity and recreation, and tourism.
