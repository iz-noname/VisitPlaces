CITIES

Take part in urban culture

Enter the streets teeming with life

Are you an urban or outdoor lover? There is a city for everyone. Plenty of cities offer a fun, lively urban environment as well as the green scenery.

A city can be shaped by many things—its food scene, for example, or abundance of shopping options. But perhaps nothing can define a place quite as distinctly as its architecture. Whether it's through thoughtful urban planning or adapting to cultural movements, the visual design of a city can transport you through time, while planting you firmly in a very specific place.
