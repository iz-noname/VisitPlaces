B&BS & GUESTHOUSES AROUND THE WORLD

Although the whole concept may sound British, B&Bs are popular all around the world.

Home hospitality

What could be better than waking up in a comfy bed inside a city townhouse, country farm cottage, luxury lodge, or even a castle, before tucking into a breakfast? It's all part of the experience at a bed and breakfast (B&B) or guest house. Whether you are exploring cities, towns or the countryside, you can almost always find a quality B&B or guest house for an unforgettable stay.

Ask your friendly host for local know-how and they'll recommend great places to visit, where to eat, and top tips on travel. You'll find plenty of homely, personal touches and often there are excellent facilities, which might include off-road parking, wonderful loch-side views, pet-friendly rooms and handy bike or golf club storage.
